About Us

Welcome to Frog Hollow Firewood! We are dedicated to providing high-quality firewood for all your heating and recreational needs. Based in Black River Falls, WI, we offer exceptional value and convenience, specializing in campground deliveries to enhance your outdoor experiences.

Services

We provide meticulously cut, split, and seasoned firewood that is ready to burn the moment it arrives. Our services include:

  • Free delivery within 20 miles of our woodyard
  • Specialized campground deliveries
  • Bulk firewood sales for cost savings
  • High-quality campfire and heating wood
  • Flexible delivery options
